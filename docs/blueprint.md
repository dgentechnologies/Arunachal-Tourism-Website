# **App Name**: Arunachal Explore

## Core Features:

- Hotel Booking Portal: Browse, filter, and book accommodation options across Arunachal Pradesh.
- Transportation Booking: Book cars, bikes, and other transportation services for travel within the region.
- Safety & Emergency Locator: Find nearest police stations, hospitals, and other emergency services based on current location.
- Digital Tour Permit Application: A streamlined platform to submit tour plans and apply for necessary permits digitally.
- AI-Assisted Permit Review Tool: An AI tool that helps review user-submitted tour plans for completeness and adherence to permit guidelines, suggesting missing information.
- AI-Powered Itinerary Planner: A tool that generates personalized trip itineraries and 'best trips' suggestions based on user interests, duration, and preferred activities.
- Curated Travel Guides: Access detailed guides for popular destinations, attractions, and cultural insights within Arunachal Pradesh.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Light and airy, inspired by the natural beauty and serene landscapes of Arunachal Pradesh.
- Primary color: A rich, earthy forest green (#417D35), representing the lush mountains and forests. This provides a natural, grounded, and inviting feel.
- Background color: A very subtle, almost imperceptible greenish off-white (#F1F4F0), designed to be gentle on the eyes and serve as a clean canvas for content.
- Accent color: A vibrant yellow-green (#B1E052), analogous to the primary color, chosen to highlight interactive elements, calls to action, and important notices, adding a touch of freshness.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, providing a modern, objective, and highly legible experience across all text, suitable for a clear information portal.
- Icons should be clean, line-based, and visually represent elements of nature, culture, and travel, maintaining a consistent, approachable style throughout the application.
- A clean, intuitive, and mobile-first responsive layout emphasizing stunning natural photography and clear hierarchical information presentation for easy navigation.
- Subtle and purposeful animations for page transitions and interactive elements, ensuring a smooth and delightful user experience without distraction.
